Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To empower youth through leadership, social service and community engagement while upholding the sacred values of Vasavi Matha.

Our Vision

Building responsible citizens dedicated to selfless service, cultural preservation and nation-building — inspired by the noble sacrifice of Vasavi Kanyaka Parameswari.

Our Values

Unity, Devotion, Brotherhood, Excellence — the four pillars that guide every action of MVYS across 20 years of service.

⚡
Empowering Youth
Developing the next generation of leaders through skill-building, mentorship, scholarships and career guidance programmes.
👑
Leadership Development
Grooming members for positions at local, district and global levels — MVYS alumni now lead at WAM, KAVM and international platforms.
🤝
Social Service
Blood donation, COVID relief ₹20L+, hospital meals, blanket drives — compassion expressed through 20 years of consistent action.
🌍
Community Engagement
Uniting families through festivals, sports tournaments, cultural events and neighbourhood outreach across Manjunathnagar.
🏛️
Cultural Preservation
Honouring Vasavi heritage through devotional activities, sacred pilgrimages to 13 kshetras and dedicated temple service.
